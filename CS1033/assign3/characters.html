Main Characters

  • Elsa

  • Anna

  • Olef

  • Kristoff
 

Elsa

The Snow Queen with the power to create ice and snow, struggling to control her abilities while protecting her kingdom of Arendelle

Anna

Elsa's spirited and fearless sister, embarking on a journey to bring Elsa back home and save their kingdom from eternal winter
 

Olef

The lovable snowman brought to life by Elsa's magic, dreaming of experiencing summer and warm hugs
 

Kristoff

The rugged ice harvester, accompanied by his loyal reindeer companion, Sven

  

Together, they form a diverse and endearing cast that brings warmth and laughter to every scene.
