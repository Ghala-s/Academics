Plot
 

In the kingdom of Arendelle, Princess Elsa harbors a powerful secret—she possesses magical ice powers. When her abilities accidentally plunge Arendelle into eternal winter, Elsa flees into the mountains, inadvertently trapping the kingdom in ice.
 

Determined to bring her sister back and save Arendelle, Princess Anna sets off on a perilous journey with the help of ice harvester Kristoff, his loyal reindeer Sven, and the optimistic snowman Olaf.
       

Along the way, they encounter mystical trolls, treacherous landscapes, and confront Elsa's fearsome magic. Through bravery, sacrifice, and the power of love, Anna discovers the true meaning of family and breaks the icy curse that has gripped Arendelle.
