Fun Facts
  • The animators traveled to Norway to study its landscapes and architecture, drawing inspiration for the fictional kingdom of Arendelle's design.

 
  • Elsa was originally conceived as a villain, but the filmmakers rewrote her character to explore themes of acceptance and self-empowerment.
  • "Let It Go," Elsa's iconic anthem of self-discovery, became a global phenomenon, winning the Academy Award for Best Original Song and earning countless covers and parodies.

If you're interested in discovering even more fun facts about Frozen, visit the official Frozen website.
