Happy 60th Anniversary!

This year, 2024, marks the 60th anniversary for the Computer Science department at Western University. This department has seen a lot of change in its six decades of existence.

The department began in 1964 and it has grown drastically since then. The department has become one of the largest departments at Western in recent years. One of its fundamental courses, CS 1026, is now taken by about 2000 students per year, many of which wishing to pursue a degree in Computer Science but some from other departments who want to learn the basics of programming as part of their Science credits.

Navigate through the pages of this website to learn more about the department's history, current course offerings, and some interesting research topics from our faculty!
